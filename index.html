Projects

Public Health GIS & Environmental Surveillance (Portage County Health District)

As a contracted GIS technician at the Portage County Health District (PCHD), I supported data-driven public health decision-making through mapping, spatial analysis, and dashboard development. My work integrated field data, environmental hazards, and chronic illness reporting into intuitive visuals for public and internal use.

COVID-19 Case Tracking Dashboard

I maintained and expanded the COVID-19 surveillance dashboard, incorporating hospitalization trends, death counts, and zip-code-based case clusters using Tableau and ArcGIS Online. Interactive components allowed real-time queries and visual summaries for public awareness.

Harmful Algal Bloom (HAB) Mapping

Created early versions of a HAB monitoring map using water sample collection sites and satellite-aided detection. The goal was to inform residents and state agencies of lake conditions and bloom severity in near real-time.

Food Establishment Risk & Inspection Maps

Used ArcGIS Pro to create heatmaps of food safety violations, aiding in field inspection planning. These maps highlighted risk clusters and frequency zones to optimize public health interventions.

Mapped risk categories (e.g., C1S, C3L) across Portage County using symbology and clustering, simplifying regulatory classification into an easily digestible format.

Icon-based visualization of retail food establishments, sorting them by type (e.g., FSO, RFE) to support licensing and zoning compliance operations.
