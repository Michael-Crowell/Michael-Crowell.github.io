About Me

I’m a climate scientist and geospatial data analyst with experience across environmental, health, and academic sectors. I specialize in making complex data understandable and actionable — whether through mapping, visualization, or automated analysis workflows. My projects have ranged from modeling tornado-favorable environments to building public health dashboards, using tools like R, Python, ArcGIS Pro, and Tableau. I have experience transforming multi-million-cell datasets and multidimensional arrays in R into highly refined, targeted visualizations. Above all, I focus on turning data into tools that people can actually use.

Projects

Public Health GIS & Environmental Surveillance – Portage County Health District

During my contract with PCHD, I applied GIS, data visualization, and statistical analysis to track infectious diseases, identify environmental health hazards, and optimize inspection workflows. I created dashboards, mapped public health infrastructure, geocoded thousands of addresses, and helped visualize regional chronic disease data using advanced spatial tools.

COVID-19 & Chronic Disease Dashboarding

Built and maintained a COVID-19 dashboard in ArcGIS Online and Tableau to track case trends, hospitalization rates, and chronic disease comorbidities. The dashboard was used for public communication and internal health surveillance.

Harmful Algal Bloom (HAB) Surveillance

Developed a HAB heatmap using water sample point data and historical bloom records. This ArcGIS Pro-based output supports ongoing public health hazard communication and environmental tracking workflows.

Food Infrastructure Mapping

Mapped restaurant inspections, food facility risk levels, and inspection density patterns using ArcGIS Pro. These outputs were used by sanitarians to improve inspection routing and food safety planning.

Geocoding & Address Standardization

Created automated workflows in ArcGIS Pro and R to geocode thousands of addresses and join them with FIPS-coded geographic units. This enabled regional analysis of food safety, COVID outbreaks, and chronic illness data by location. These workflows supported all other spatial visualizations in this section.

Tools: ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, R, Tableau, Python, Spatial Analyst, Geocoding APIs
